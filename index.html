About Us

Who We Are

We are a family run business based on the out skirts of Limerick City. We pride ourselves on results and ensure a motivational and positive environment. With a number of employees with qualifications of all levels of fitness and nutrition background we can help you achieve any goals you may have.

Our Facilities

  • State of the art equipment, free weights, weight machines, treadmills and many more
  • Cardio Area
  • A studio room for all our classes
  • Healthy meals and drinks can be ordered at reception
  • Men and women changing rooms with showers
  • Free Parking on site
